# Broccoli Slaw

### Ingredients

* 5 cups chopped broccoli stems and florets
* 1 cup shredded carrots
* 1 cup sliced red cabbage
* ¼ cup chopped red onion
* ½ lb cooked bacon, crumbled
* 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
* Salt and pepper, to taste
* Raisins, optional

#### Dressing

* 1 cup mayonnaise
* ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
* 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
* 3 tbsp sugar (or monk fruit sweetener)

### Directions

1. Combine broccoli, carrots, cabbage, red onion, bacon, and cheddar in a large bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
2. Whisk together mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, and sugar until smooth.
3. Pour dressing over slaw and toss to coat. _(Dressing recipe can be doubled or tripled depending on how wet you prefer the salad.)_
4. Let sit in the refrigerator for a few hours before serving to allow flavors to meld. Do not let sit overnight.
