# Sweet Baked Brussels Sprouts

Servings: 4 | Temp: 400°F | Cook: 25–30 min

### Ingredients

* 1½ lbs Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
* 3 tbsp olive oil
* 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
* 1 tbsp maple syrup (or honey)
* 1 tbsp brown sugar
* ½ tsp salt
* ¼ tsp black pepper
* Optional: ¼ cup chopped pecans or walnuts
* Optional: ¼ cup dried cranberries

### Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, maple syrup, and brown sugar.
3. Add Brussels sprouts and toss to coat evenly.
4. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Roast 25–30 minutes, stirring halfway through, until caramelized and crispy on the edges.
6. In the last 5 minutes, sprinkle nuts and/or cranberries over the top if using.
7. Serve hot.
