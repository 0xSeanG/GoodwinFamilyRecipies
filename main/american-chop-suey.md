# American Chop Suey

Source: Family recipe (Love Gramp)

### Ingredients

* ⅓ to ½ lb ground beef (hamburg)
* ½ of a medium to large onion, cut into fairly large pieces
* 1 medium carrot, diced
* 2 garlic cloves, pressed and cut into small pieces
* 1 stalk celery, cut into ¼-inch slices
* ½ red bell pepper, cut into 1 x ¼-inch pieces
* ½ green bell pepper, same treatment
* 3 or 4 mushrooms, sliced
* 1 tomato, cut into medium pieces (or ½ can whole tomatoes)
* 1 handful fresh spinach, optional
* 1 cup elbow macaroni
* 1 can Campbell's tomato condensed soup
* 3 tbsp olive oil
* Salt, pepper, and cumin, to taste
* A little red wine

### Directions

1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over fairly high heat.
2. Add ground beef and onion. Cook until onions are translucent. Pour in a little red wine.
3. Add garlic and carrot. Cook another 2–3 minutes.
4. _(Start boiling salted water for the pasta now.)_
5. Add everything else except the spinach (peppers, celery, mushrooms, tomato). Continue cooking over medium heat.
6. Add pasta to the boiling water and set a timer for 9 minutes.
7. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and cumin over the pan.
8. After 4 minutes, add the can of tomato soup and stir.
9. After 2 more minutes, add the spinach.
10. When the timer goes off, drain the pasta and add to the pan.
11. Stir and serve.
